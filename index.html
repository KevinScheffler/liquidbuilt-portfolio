Client workHealth & Wellness

Made the subscription discount show up instantly, not after the widget loaded.

Zivolife's product page first displayed the full one-time price, then flickered to the discounted subscription price after the Recharge widget loaded. That created unnecessary doubt at the point of purchase. After working with Recharge support to understand the widget's rendering behavior, I built a Liquid and JavaScript override that displays the subscription price from the first paint and switches only when a shopper selects "Buy once." No additional app required.

An upsell that validates input before it ever hits the cart.

A Shopify theme seller needed a checkbox upsell on the product page. When selected, a second product is added alongside the main item, but only after the customer completes a required field. I rebuilt the buy-button snippet to validate the input, stop the add-to-cart action with a clear error when it is empty, and leave the standard flow untouched when the upsell is not selected. Minimal vanilla JavaScript, scoped CSS, and no additional app.
